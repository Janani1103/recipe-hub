Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Butter for ramekins
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • Vanilla ice cream (for serving)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Butter 4 ramekins and dust with cocoa powder
  3. Melt butter and chocolate in double boiler
  4. In separate bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and salt until pale
  5. Fold melted chocolate into egg mixture
  6. Sift flour into mixture and fold gently
  7. Divide batter among prepared ramekins
  8. Bake for 12-14 minutes until edges are firm but center is soft
  9. Let cool for 1 minute, then invert onto plates
  10. Serve immediately with vanilla ice cream

Classic New York Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • For Crust:
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • For Filling:
  • 4 packages (8oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C)
  2. Mix crust ingredients and press into 9-inch springform pan
  3. Bake crust for 10 minutes, then cool
  4. Beat cream cheese until smooth
  5. Gradually add sugar, then sour cream and vanilla
  6. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each
  7. Mix in flour until just combined
  8. Pour filling over crust and smooth top
  9. Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes until center is almost set
  10. Turn off oven and let cheesecake cool in oven for 1 hour
  11. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving
