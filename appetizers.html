Classic Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mozzarella cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)
  2. Arrange baguette slices on baking sheet and toast for 5-7 minutes until golden
  3. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  5. Rub toasted bread slices with garlic clove
  6. Spoon tomato mixture onto bread slices
  7. Add mozzarella if desired and serve immediately

Garlic Parmesan Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 20 large white mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup cream cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Clean mushrooms and remove stems
  3. Chop mushroom stems finely
  4. Heat olive oil in pan, sauté garlic and chopped stems for 5 minutes
  5. In a bowl, mix cream cheese, Parmesan, breadcrumbs, parsley, and sautéed mixture
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Stuff mushroom caps with the mixture
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown
  9. Garnish with extra parsley and serve warm
